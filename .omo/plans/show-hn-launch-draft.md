## HN Guidelines Recap

- ❌ **Do not** ask anyone to upvote you. HN's ring detection is excellent.
- ❌ **Do not** post the link to friends — their upvotes won't count.
- ✅ **Do** reply to every comment in the thread. Engagement keeps posts alive.
- ✅ **Do** be humble and honest about what the site does and doesn't do.
- ✅ **Do** accept criticism gracefully — HN users are brutally honest but it's the best feedback you'll get.

## Timing

- **Best time**: Tuesday–Thursday, morning US time (8–10 AM Eastern / 12–14 UTC).
- **Why**: More HN users are active. Monday posts get buried by weekend content. Friday–Sunday posts have less attention.

## After posting

1. **Keep refreshing** — reply to every comment within the first 2 hours
2. **Fix bugs immediately** — if someone reports a bug, fix it while the thread is active
3. **Monitor analytics** — watch for traffic spikes
4. **Save the thread** — if it gets flagged or killed, don't repost; wait a month
